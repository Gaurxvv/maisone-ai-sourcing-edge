# Ideas to extend the Maisone prototype

The site already covers Hero, Features, Dashboard, AI Assistant, Marketplace, Portals, Analytics, Sourcing Network, Global Presence, Automation, How It Works, Testimonials, Pricing, Book Demo. Below are high-leverage additions that reinforce the "luxury AI fashion-tech" narrative without redesigning anything.

## Tier 1 — Investor & credibility

1. **Case Studies / Success Stories**
   Three editorial cards (e.g. "How a Tokyo denim label scaled to 14 markets in 90 days") with metric callouts (lead time ↓ 42%, MOQ ↓ 60%, margin ↑ 18%). Click → expanded story modal.

2. **Press & Investors strip**
   Logos of fictional but believable VCs and press ("Backed by", "As featured in: Vogue Business, BoF, WWD, Nikkei").

3. **Trust & Compliance**
   GOTS, OEKO-TEX, BSCI, GDPR, SOC 2 badges with short copy on ethical sourcing, traceability and audit trail.

4. **Sustainability / Impact dashboard**
   Live-style counters: CO₂ saved, water saved, % verified ethical mills, traceability score per order.

## Tier 2 — Product depth

5. **AI Trend Forecasting module**
   A visual board: trending fabrics, colors (Pantone-style chips), silhouettes by region, with confidence scores and source signals (runway, social, retail).

6. **Supplier Profile preview**
   One detailed mock supplier card opened from the Marketplace: capacity calendar, certifications, past clients, sample gallery, response time, risk score.

7. **RFQ / Sample Request flow mock**
   A 3-step inline mock (Brief → AI Match → Quote comparison table) showing 4 suppliers side-by-side with price, MOQ, lead time, sustainability score.

8. **Logistics Tracker**
   Animated shipment timeline (Mill → QC → Port → Customs → Warehouse → Brand) with ETA and risk alerts.

9. **Integrations grid**
   Shopify, Zoho, NetSuite, SAP, Stripe, DHL, Maersk, Slack, Notion — "Plug Maisone into your stack".

## Tier 3 — Brand & engagement

10. **Editorial / Journal section** ("Maisone Edit") — 3 magazine-style article cards on craftsmanship, AI in fashion, sourcing trends.
11. **FAQ** — accordion answering brands' common sourcing questions.
12. **Careers teaser** — "Building the operating system for global fashion. We're hiring across Tokyo, London, Paris, NYC."
13. **Newsletter / Waitlist** — luxe single-input capture, "Join 4,200 founders & sourcing leads".
14. **Founders / Team section** — 3–4 portraits with short bios and provenance (LVMH, Uniqlo, Stripe, Google DeepMind).
15. **Multi-region switcher** in nav (JP / UK / EU / US) with localized currency + language hint — purely visual.
16. **Live activity ticker** ("Atelier Camden accepted RFQ #4821 · 2s ago") fixed bottom-left, mockup only.

## Recommendation

If you want maximum investor impact with minimum bloat, add **3–4** in this order:

1. Case Studies
2. Press & Investors logos + Trust badges (combined strip)
3. AI Trend Forecasting module
4. Supplier Profile preview (linked from Marketplace)

All purely frontend, matching the existing glassmorphic dark aesthetic, framer-motion only, no backend.
